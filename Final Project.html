Final Project

This webpage is dedicated to my final project content for CSC 106. I chose to create a game, called "Pizza Delivery." The premise of the game is simple: deliver the pizza. The characers within the game are heavily based on my own experience as a delivery driver. The game instructions are explained within the game itself, but they are breifly as follows:
  • There are two levels, Cross the screen to complete each level and get a tip.
  • The character moves across the screen automatically, but you control up / down movement
  • The enemies are from a rival restaurant called YBL and want to steal your recipes
  • Click to shoot them or avoid them with the up and down arrow keys
  • If they touch you, you stop moving for 3 seconds
  • If they reach the left side of the screen, they steal a recipe
  • Your recipes are your health
  • If they reach 0, the game ends
  • Your tips are your score
  • Tips are calcuated based on how many recipes you have left when you reach the right side and how long it took you to get there
Good luck!
